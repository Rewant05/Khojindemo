Welcome to KHOJ

Your trusted companion in finding what matters most.

Our Mission

KHOJ is revolutionizing the way India handles lost and found. We're building the nation's first centralized digital platform for reporting and reclaiming lost items — from metro stations to shopping malls.

How It Works

  • Report: Share your lost or found item with key details.
  • Match: Let our intelligent system find potential matches.
  • Retrieve: Reclaim your item with proper proof.

AI-Powered Verification

We ensure your reports go through secure verification using AI & image recognition to reduce fraud and build a trustworthy ecosystem.

Why It Matters

Each year, countless personal belongings are lost in public places. KHOJ ensures they aren’t lost forever — providing peace of mind, reducing police workload, and uniting people with what’s rightfully theirs.

Support the Movement

Be a part of India’s first national Lost & Found platform. Help us expand, innovate and reach every corner of the country.
