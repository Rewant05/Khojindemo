KHOJ Frontend Bundle

Files included:
- index.html
- login.html
- register.html
- report-lost.html
- report-found.html
- dashboard.html
- admin.html
- about.html
- contact.html
- styles.css
- app.js

How to use:
1. Put all files inside your frontend folder, or later inside the Express public/ folder.
2. Open index.html to preview.
3. When backend is ready, connect forms and buttons to Express routes.
