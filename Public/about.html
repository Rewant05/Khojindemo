Why KHOJ exists

Lost & found in India is still too fragmented.

People often lose belongings in metros, campuses, malls, airports, events, hotels, or offices. The problem is not just losing the item — it is not knowing where to begin searching. KHOJ is designed as a central digital layer that connects users with lost & found systems and improves recovery visibility.

Single place to report lost or found items
Cleaner matching between user reports and center logs
More trustworthy handover and claim journey
Scalable model for campuses, transit hubs, and institutions

FAQ

About the platform

Helpful for judges, users, and partners who want quick clarity.

Is KHOJ just for individuals?

No. KHOJ is useful for both individuals and institutions like campuses, transit hubs, and commercial spaces that handle lost items.

How is this different from a simple lost-item board?

KHOJ is designed as a structured product with matching, status workflows, user dashboards, and future center integrations.

Can this become a real startup?

Yes. The problem is real, scalable, and has strong civic-tech as well as product-led startup potential if executed well.
