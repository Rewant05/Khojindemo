How it works

A simple 4-step journey from report to recovery.

The experience should feel easy even when the situation is stressful. That is why the flow stays direct, guided, and verification-first.

01

Report the item

Add the item name, photo, date, location, and supporting details in a guided form.

02

Match reports

KHOJ compares lost and found entries using item details, images, and geography.

03

Verify ownership

The platform supports proof-based claims before any item is handed over.

04

Recover securely

Users coordinate with the relevant center or finder to complete retrieval more safely.

FAQ

Questions users and partners may ask first.

Good product websites answer hesitation before it becomes friction.

How does KHOJ help recover lost items?

Users submit lost or found reports with images, descriptions, and locations. KHOJ then helps surface likely matches and routes the next steps more clearly.

Who can use the platform?

Individuals, colleges, malls, stations, airports, event venues, and dedicated lost & found centers can all use KHOJ.

Why does the design look more professional than futuristic?

Because trust, clarity, and speed matter more than flashy visuals for users who are already stressed about losing something important.
