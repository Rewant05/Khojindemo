How it works

A simple 4-step journey from report to recovery.

The experience should feel easy even when the situation is stressful. That is why the flow stays direct, guided, and verification-first.

01

Report the item

Add the item name, photo, date, location, and supporting details in a guided form.

02

Match reports

KHOJ compares lost and found entries using item details, images, and geography.

03

Verify ownership

The platform supports proof-based claims before any item is handed over.

04

Recover securely

Users coordinate with the relevant center or finder to complete retrieval more safely.

FAQ

Questions users and partners may ask first.

Good homepage design answers hesitation before it becomes friction.

How does KHOJ help users recover lost items?

Users can report lost or found items, upload images, add location and time details, and receive updates when a possible match is detected.

Who can use this platform?

Individuals, colleges, transport hubs, malls, event venues, and any lost & found center can use KHOJ to manage reports more efficiently.

Is the platform only for individuals?

No. KHOJ is designed for both consumers and institutions, making it useful as a central network for centers across India.

Why is the design clean instead of futuristic?

Because trust, clarity, and speed matter more than flashy effects for people who are already stressed about losing something important.
